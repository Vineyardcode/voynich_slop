# NE, SE, SW and NW corners, are four smaller circles. In the center of the
# large circle is a square, within which is inscribed another circle, within

# come in towards the square from the perimeter of the large circle in the NE,
# NW, SE and SW direction. Each of these eight lines are terminated with a
# branching broccoli-like structure.
# The small circles in the corners contain between one and four human faces,
# joined by lines.
# There is a "sentence'' of Voynich text above, below, to the left and the
# right of the large circle. The bottom left small circle contains a "word''
